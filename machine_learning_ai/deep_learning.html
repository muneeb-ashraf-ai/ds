Deep Learning

An Artificial Neural Network (ANN) is a computational model inspired by the structure and function of biological neural networks. ANNs are composed of interconnected nodes, or "neurons," organized in layers. Each connection has a weight that is adjusted during the training process. ANNs are the foundation of deep learning and are used for a wide variety of tasks, including classification and regression.
