Discrete Mathematics

The study of mathematical structures that are fundamentally discrete rather than continuous.

Overview

Discrete mathematics is the study of mathematical structures that are countable or otherwise distinct and separable. Examples of discrete mathematical objects include integers, graphs, and statements in logic.

  • Logic and Proofs
  • Number Theory
